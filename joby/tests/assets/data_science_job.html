Introduction
Bei einem unserer namhaften Kunden in Penzberg, einem internationalen Biotech-Unternehmen, bietet sich diese interessante Perspektive: Scientific Data Manager (m/w) Kennziffer 42974
Description
Ihre Aufgaben

- You contribute to study protocols, analysis plans and reports and support Companion Diagnostics projects.
- You are responsible for acquisition of clinical and laboratory data, set-up and testing of respective data bases.
- You ensure the quality of acquired data and the electronic transfer to biostatistics, regulatory bodies and external partners.
- You set-up Data Management plans, Case Report Forms, and study level documentation
- You further develop existing data management systems
- You develop data management processes, SOPs, and guidelines.
- You communicate between study managers, study monitors, medical doctors and biostatisticians.
- You collaborate with Pharma partners to ensure CDx data requirements are achieved in the clinical trial.
- You recruit, train, coach and support new staff
- You promote CDM excellence by facilitating knowledge sharing and creating an environment of continuous improvement

Ihr Profil

- Education as medical documentation specialist or a university degree in mathematics, statistics, medicine or pharmacy
- Professional experience as Data Manager, prior experience managing Companion Diagnostics projects is preferred
- Experience with patient data in a GxP regulated environment, familiar with quality insurance, risk management, compliance and most relevant data formats as well as knowledge of relevant standards (GCP-ICH, CDISC).
- Good knowledge of data bases and SQL.
- Good knowledge of a statistical programming language (R or SAS) desirable.
- Strong communication skills
- Enjoying to work in interdisciplinary and international project teams
- Willigness to travel
